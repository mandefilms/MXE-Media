On Set

How We Work On Location

We operate as a field production team supporting marketing agencies, production companies, and in-house creative teams. We work from your creative brief, shot list, or storyboard — or develop one with you based on your objectives.

Our two-person team handles directing, camera operation, lighting, audio, drone, and DIT simultaneously — with no communication lag between creative decision and technical execution. One team, one vision, zero confusion.

We're experienced with approval workflows, brand compliance reviews, remote direction via video link, and same-day turnaround when the timeline requires it.

Organized Footage

All footage delivered organized by scene, labeled by camera angle and take, with matching audio files synced and ready for your editor.

Technical Deliverables

ProRes, H.265, or raw formats to your spec. Color-matched multi-cam footage, broadcast-safe levels, and frame-accurate timecode across all cameras.

Same-Day Selects

For time-sensitive projects, we provide same-day selects, proxy files, or rough assemblies uploaded before we leave the location.
